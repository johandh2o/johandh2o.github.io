About me

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences (MatNat) at the University of Oslo (UiO), under the supervision of Dr. Johan Pensar. Concurrently, I am a researcher at the Norwegian Institute of Public Health (NIPH), under the supervision of Dr. Guido Biele.

I hold baccalaureate degrees in industrial engineering and in economics, from Universidad de los Andes (UniAndes) in Colombia; plus master’s degrees in economics and in statistics, the latter from Michigan State University (MSU). I am an international Fulbright scholar (2019).

Research

My research interests, accumulated knowledge and experience gravitate around:

  • Causal inference with observational data

  • Epistemic uncertainty quantification for machine learning and causal inference

  • Statistics + game theory: strategic complements networks, and dynamic treatment regimes
